Our mission is to help woman create their own fashion, feel beautiful, inspired and spread good vibes.

Designed Differently

Our difference is how our patterns look. Sewing patterns don’t have to look like NASA blueprints and that is why we threw out the rule book and injected a sense of design and simplicity.

PERFECT FOR BEGINNERS

Made Label is for everyone that always dreamed of sewing their own wardrobes but weren’t sure where to start. Our step by step sew-along tutorials make sewing easy for makers of any ability.

easy style & TIMELESS CHIC

Made Label focuses on timeless pieces inspired by the effortless style of Parisian fashion and the relaxed bohemian spirit of Australian summers.

Made to mix & Match

We know once you find a pattern that fits, you want to make it again. With our mix and match collections you can sew the same top or skirt in new combinations, creating new beautiful garments and refining your skills.

And... Doing good in the world

We believe in doing good in the world and this creators’ community is full of support, encouragement and kindess. With every pattern, we give back to the Batwa Pygmy tribe in Uganda... one community helping another!
